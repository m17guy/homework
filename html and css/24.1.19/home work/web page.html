Borderlands 3 – What we know so far:

Borderlands is a series of action role-playing first-person shooter video games set in space.
The game was created by Gearbox Software and published by 2K Games.
The series currently consists of three games – Borderlands (2009), Borderlands 2 (2012) and Borderlands: The Pre-Sequel (2014).
While Borderlands 3 has’t been officially confirmed, another game in the series is pretty a given.
Rumours have been swimming around for a while now, in the wake of Borderlands 2’s massive success.
Gearbox CEO Randy Pitchford said at PAX East 2016: “It’s no secret, obviously there’s going to be another Borderlands.”
So while it may not be called Borderland 3 – there will definitely be another game.
